Project Objective: Amazon Customer Shopping Analysis

The objective of this project is to **analyse customer shopping behaviour** and provide **actionable insights** to support business decision-making. The analysis focuses on three main areas:

## Key Goals
1. **Purchase Trends:** Identify top-selling products, categories, and seasonal trends.  
2. **Customer Segmentation:** Segment customers based on purchase history, subscription status, and spending patterns.  
3. **Marketing Opportunities:** Recommend strategies for promotions, subscription growth, and loyalty programs.
